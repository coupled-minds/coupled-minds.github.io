Research

How do minds align in conversation? How do we transmit thoughts from one brain to another? How do we coordinate behavior?

We study how people explore ideas, align opinions, and resolve conflict through conversation. Our work integrates fMRI hyperscanning, natural language processing, and computational modeling to understand how alignment emerges in real-world interactions and how this differs in human–AI dialogue.
